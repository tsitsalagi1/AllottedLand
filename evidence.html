People Powered Evidence Project

How was allotted land lost?

A voluntary, source-linked project to document patterns families find in county, federal, tribal, and archival records.

What this project is

The goal is to let families voluntarily report what their records show after allotment: tax sale, mortgage foreclosure, sheriff sale, private deed, probate/partition, guardian sale, right-of-way, fraud allegation, restriction-removal issue, or still unknown.

Submissions are reviewed before any individual story or data point is published. Public charts should show aggregate patterns, not private family information.

What the data can show

  • Common land-loss categories
  • Patterns by decade
  • Patterns by county and tribe/Nation
  • How many cases are still unknown or need more records
  • Where families need county, BIA/LTRO, NARA, probate, or tribal records next
