AllottedLand Grid Calibrator v0.21

Local tool for manually aligning the outside township section lines first, then internal section lines. Nothing uploads anywhere.

1. Load the full township/range map image

Use the full source map image, usually under data/ocr_runs/source_images/cherokee_nation_loc_page_###.jpg. Do not use a section crop here.

Workflow: drag the four outside boundary lines until they sit on the outside section grid. Then click Distribute internal lines. After that, move any individual internal line that is off. Outer lines are thicker.
