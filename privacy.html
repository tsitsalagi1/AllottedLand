AllottedLand.com · Beta / Phase 1

Privacy Policy

Starter privacy policy for the public beta. Have counsel review before accepting public submissions.

Last updated: June 28, 2026. Contact email: allottedland@gmail.com. This policy describes the current beta website, public search tools, reviewer/admin tools, testimonial submissions, and People Powered Evidence Project submissions.

1. Purpose

AllottedLand.com is a free public research index and educational tool intended to help Native families locate historical allotment maps, records, research paths, and source links.

2. Information currently collected

The public search and research-path tools require the user to check agreement to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use before the buttons activate. Search clues entered into the public search form are used to return results and should not include sensitive living-person private information.

The Workbench, testimonial form, and People Powered Evidence Project form may collect human-entered record fields, reviewer initials/contact if provided, testimonial text, evidence summaries, source clues, consent confirmations, and standard technical request information such as date/time and user-agent data needed to operate and secure the service.

3. Submissions and review

Submitted records, testimonials, and evidence-project entries are sent to review queues. They are not intended to be automatically published without review. Approved public records may display names and map/record information from historical sources. Testimonials and evidence-project entries are published or aggregated only according to the submitter’s consent choice and after review.

4. Sensitive information

Users should not submit Social Security numbers, private addresses of living people, birth certificates, government IDs, financial account numbers, unredacted medical records, private information about living minors, or private documents belonging to others without permission. Public submissions require permission and consent confirmations before submit buttons activate.

5. Children and minors

AllottedLand.com is not directed to children under 13. Public testimonial and evidence submissions require a confirmation that the submitter is at least 13 years old, or that a parent/guardian is submitting and will not provide the child’s private contact information. If you believe a child’s private information was submitted, contact allottedland@gmail.com for removal review.

6. Publication choices

Future testimonial or document submissions should offer clear choices: public with name, public anonymously, private/statistical-only, or private assistance-only with no publication.

7. Aggregated data

The land-loss dashboard should use aggregated statistics whenever possible and should not identify a specific family or living person unless there is express consent.

8. Removal and corrections

Correction and removal requests may be sent to allottedland@gmail.com. Do not email sensitive private documents unless they are redacted and you have permission to share them.

9. Third-party sources

The site links to outside sources such as the Library of Congress, National Archives, county records, BIA resources, and other public or archival systems. Those sites have their own privacy rules.

10. No official government system

AllottedLand.com is independent and is not an official tribal, federal, BIA, NARA, county, or court record system.

Testimonials and evidence submissions

AllottedLand.com may collect testimonial text, evidence-project summaries, contact information provided for follow-up, consent choices, and source-record clues submitted through the website. Public display requires review and approval; source images used in the Workbench are not uploaded by the site.
