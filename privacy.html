Privacy

Privacy Policy

The short version: search is for research leads, submissions require consent, and living-person private information should not be submitted.

Last updated: June 28, 2026. Contact: allottedland@gmail.com.

What the site collects

  • Search information you enter, so the site can build results and printable packets.
  • Submission information you choose to send through testimonials, Land Loss Project forms, or reviewer tools.
  • Basic technical request information needed to operate and protect the site.

What not to submit

Do not submit Social Security numbers, private addresses of living people, government IDs, financial records, medical records, private information about living minors, or unredacted private family documents.

What becomes public

Searches are not automatically published. Testimonials and Land Loss Project entries go to review first. Public display depends on the submitter’s consent choice and review for privacy and relevance.

Corrections and removal

Send correction, anonymization, or removal requests to allottedland@gmail.com. Include the page or record involved and what needs to be corrected or removed.

Outside websites

AllottedLand.com links to outside source systems such as NARA, OHS, LOC, Federal Register, Census, county offices, BIA/LTRO, and BLM/GLO. Those sites have their own privacy rules.
