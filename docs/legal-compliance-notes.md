# Legal / compliance notes for AllottedLand.com

This is an operational checklist, not legal advice.

## Current safeguards added in v0.31

- Public search and guided research-path buttons are disabled until users confirm they agree to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
- Testimonial, evidence, and Workbench submission buttons are disabled until required consent boxes are checked.
- Backend submission APIs reject missing consent confirmations.
- Public testimonial/evidence submissions include an age/guardian confirmation.
- Policies warn users not to submit sensitive living-person information, child private information, Social Security numbers, government IDs, private addresses, medical/financial records, or documents they do not have permission to share.
- Submissions go to review queues before public display.

## Issues for counsel / admin review

- Privacy policy coverage for all jurisdictions where users may live.
- COPPA / child-privacy exposure if the site ever knowingly collects data from children under 13.
- Accessibility review, including keyboard navigation, labels, contrast, and screen-reader behavior.
- Data retention and deletion procedure for pending/approved submissions.
- Moderation procedure for removal/correction requests.
- Whether the site is nonprofit/public-interest, and what that means for state privacy laws.
- Whether email/contact information should be deleted after review.
