Before you search

Do not worry if you only know one thing. A name, roll number, county, cemetery, old town, address, deed note, or family story can start the search.

  • Put names, spelling variants, relatives, and tribe/Nation in the family fields.
  • Put roll, census-card, allotment, township/range/section, county, and address details in the land fields.
  • Use land-loss fields for tax sale, sheriff sale, probate, guardianship, mortgage, deed, oil/gas, or court information.
