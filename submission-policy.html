Before you submit

Submission Consent Policy

Submissions are voluntary. The site should only receive information you have permission to share.

What can be submitted

Families may submit testimonials, Land Loss Project information, correction requests, or reviewed map-record data when those forms are open. Submissions go to review before public display.

Consent choices

Submission forms require checkboxes before anything is sent. Depending on the form, you may choose whether information can be public, anonymous, statistical-only, or used only for review/follow-up.

Do not submit sensitive private information

Do not submit private living-person information unless you have permission. Redact private IDs, private addresses, financial records, medical records, and information about living minors.

Withdraw or correct a submission

To ask for correction, anonymization, withdrawal, or removal, contact allottedland@gmail.com.

No proof by publication

A published story or evidence entry is still only a research lead. It does not prove ownership, title, heirship, citizenship, enrollment, or legal rights.
