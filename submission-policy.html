Submission Consent Policy

Starter consent rules for testimonials, corrections, family stories, and future land-loss data submissions.

Last updated: June 28, 2026.

1. What can be submitted

The beta website can accept Workbench record submissions, testimonials, and People Powered Evidence Project entries. Submissions go to review queues before public display. The project email, allottedland@gmail.com, is for questions, corrections, removal requests, and volunteer coordination. Do not email or submit sensitive private documents unless they are redacted and you have permission to share them.

2. Consent choices required

Before submitting a story, record, correction, or land-loss entry, users must check the required Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, Submission Consent, permission, and age/guardian confirmations. Testimonial submitters should choose one of these publication options:

  • Publish my testimonial with my name.
  • Publish my testimonial anonymously.
  • Use my information only for private research or aggregate statistics.
  • Use only to help me; do not publish or include in statistics.

3. Review before publication

No submitted testimonial, document, correction, or family record should be posted publicly without review for consent, privacy, relevance, source support, and sensitive information.

4. Sensitive information warning

Users should redact Social Security numbers, private addresses of living people, government ID numbers, birth certificates, financial records, medical records, private information about living minors, and other sensitive living-person information before submitting anything. Submissions containing sensitive information may be rejected, redacted, anonymized, or removed.

5. Land-loss data categories

Future submissions may include documented categories such as tax sale, mortgage foreclosure, sheriff sale, private deed, guardian sale, minor/incompetent allottee sale, probate or partition, oil/gas or mineral severance, right-of-way, condemnation, fraud allegation, restriction removal, federal approval issue, county/state taxation issue, unknown, or other.

6. Aggregated dashboard

Charts and dashboards should generally use aggregated data. Individual family details should not be published unless the submitter expressly consents.

7. Correction and withdrawal

Submitters should be able to request correction, withdrawal, anonymization, or removal of their own public submission by contacting allottedland@gmail.com.

8. No proof of ownership

A published story or land-loss entry does not prove ownership, title, heirship, citizenship, or legal rights. It is a research lead and evidence index only.

Testimonials and evidence consent

Before submitting a testimonial or People Powered Evidence Project entry, users must agree to the Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, and Submission Consent Policy and must not submit sensitive information about living people without permission.
