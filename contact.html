Contact the project

Use the project email for questions, corrections, removal requests, and volunteer coordination. Do not email sensitive private documents.

Email

allottedland@gmail.com

Open email app Open Gmail

What to include

  • Your question or correction request.
  • The page or record involved, if you know it.
  • Ancestor name, tribe/Nation, roll number, card number, township/range/section, county, or source link if relevant.
  • Whether you want a correction, removal, volunteer information, or general help.

Do not send sensitive private documents

Email is not a secure upload system. Do not send Social Security numbers, private addresses of living people, government IDs, financial records, medical records, or unredacted private family documents.
