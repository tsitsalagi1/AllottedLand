Contact

A privacy-first contact process will be added before public submissions or uploads open.

Do not send private family documents yet.

The current site does not have a secure upload or intake system. Please do not send Social Security numbers, private addresses of living people, birth certificates, government IDs, medical records, financial records, or unredacted private family documents through any public channel.

Contact options to add next

Before accepting submissions, the project should add a dedicated contact method for:

  • Corrections to map or source-record information.
  • Removal requests.
  • Volunteer indexing help.
  • Questions about how to use the site.
  • Consent-based testimonials and land-loss data submissions.

What a future correction should include

Corrections should include a source link or source description whenever possible, such as a LOC map page, Dawes/NARA record, county record book/page, BIA/LTRO record, or tribal archive citation.

A good correction includes: ancestor name, tribe/Nation, roll or enrollment number if known, legal description if known, source record, and what needs to be corrected.

Privacy rule

Public corrections and stories should not identify living people or reveal private family information unless the person submitting has permission and has expressly consented to publication.
