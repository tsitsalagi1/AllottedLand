Terms of Use

Starter terms for the public beta. Have counsel review before accepting public submissions.

Last updated: June 25, 2026.

1. Research index only

AllottedLand.com is a free research index and educational tool. It helps users locate possible records, maps, source links, and research paths.

2. No legal determinations

The site does not determine ownership, title, heirship, citizenship, enrollment, probate rights, mineral rights, trust status, restriction status, land recovery claims, or legal rights.

3. No legal advice

Information on the site is not legal advice and does not create an attorney-client relationship. Users should consult qualified legal counsel, tribal officials, BIA/LTRO offices, county clerks, NARA, courts, or other appropriate authorities when needed.

4. Verify original records

Search results may be incomplete, unverified, OCR-derived, user-submitted, or based on partial information. Users must verify all findings against original records and official sources.

5. Responsible use

Users agree not to submit private information about living people without permission, not to impersonate others, not to submit knowingly false information, and not to misuse family records.

6. Source links

Source links may point to outside systems. AllottedLand.com is not responsible for the availability, accuracy, or policies of outside websites.

7. Corrections

Questions, correction requests, and removal requests may be sent to allottedland@gmail.com. The project should maintain a correction process for disputed names, spelling variants, land descriptions, source links, and publication issues once submissions open.

8. Independent project

AllottedLand.com is independent and is not an official tribal, federal, BIA, NARA, county, or court record system.
